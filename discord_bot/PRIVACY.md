# Privacy Policy

Bot of Greed collects and stores the following data to operate:

- Your server ID, to identify where notifications should be sent
- The channel ID(s) you configure, to know where to post
- Your server's preferences, including notification language and which notification types are enabled

This data is used solely to deliver the service and is never sold, shared, or used for any other purpose. You can request deletion of your server's data at any time by removing the bot from your server or by contacting the author directly.
No personal user data (usernames, messages, user IDs) is collected or stored.
